🧠 Core setup (assume this always exists)

You’re tracking two people: A and B

Define once:

# distances
d(A_wrist, B_head)
d(A_wrist, B_torso)
d(A_ankle, B_hip)

# velocities
v(A_wrist), v(A_ankle), v(A_hip)

# angles
angle(A_elbow), angle(A_knee)

# orientation
vec(A_shoulders), vec(A_hips)

# meta
inter_person_distance

Everything below is built on this.

⚔️ 1. PUNCH / STRIKE DETECTION
🎯 Straight punch (random street version included)
Signal pattern:
v(A_wrist) ↑↑ (spike)
angle(A_elbow) → extend (~160–180°)
d(A_wrist, B_head or B_upper_body) ↓ rapidly
shoulder rotation ↑
Extra street-fight realism:
trajectory NOT clean (no perfect straight line)
multiple rapid spikes (flurry)
Robust condition:
if v(wrist) > T1 
and elbow_extension > T2 
and d(wrist, opponent_upper) < T3:
    event = "strike_attempt"
🔁 Wild swinging (non-trained punches)
Signal:
v(wrist) high
trajectory = curved / inconsistent
shoulder movement exaggerated
balance unstable (hip jitter ↑)

👉 Key difference from normal motion:

repeated chaotic bursts
🛡️ 2. GUARD / DEFENSIVE POSTURE
Signal:
d(wrist, own_face) ↓
elbows tucked (angle ↓)
symmetry between hands ↑
movement minimal but reactive
Add reaction:
if opponent_wrist_velocity ↑:
    own_head displacement ↑

👉 That’s defensive flinch response

🤼 3. GRABBING / HOLDING
🎯 Wrist-to-body grab
Signal:
d(A_wrist, B_torso) ↓↓↓ (sustained, not spike)
v(A_wrist) ↓ after contact
both persons' hip velocities correlate ↑

👉 Important:

NOT a spike → a hold
🎯 Collar / neck grab
Signal:
d(A_wrist, B_head/neck) ↓
wrist stays near head
B_head displacement controlled by A
🎯 Two-hand pushing hold
Signal:
both wrists near opponent torso
arms semi-extended
forward force from hips
🫷 4. PUSHING
Signal:
both wrists move toward opponent torso
v(A_hip) forward ↑
B_hip displacement backward ↑
arms extend but slower than punch
Key difference from punch:
lower velocity
longer contact duration
🦵 5. KICKING (REALISTIC, NOT MMA CLEAN)
🎯 Random street kick
Signal:
v(A_ankle) ↑↑
knee extension ↑
foot height ↑
trajectory inconsistent
balance unstable (hip wobble ↑)
Contact zone:
d(A_ankle, B_lower_body or torso) ↓

👉 Street kicks:

often low (legs, hips)
messy trajectory
followed by imbalance
🎯 Push kick (like “get away”)
Signal:
leg extends forward
v moderate (not explosive)
contact sustained briefly
B moves backward
🧨 6. CLOSE-RANGE CHAOTIC FIGHT (MOST IMPORTANT)

This is what real fights mostly look like.

Signal cluster:
inter_person_distance ↓↓↓
multiple wrist velocity spikes (both A & B)
hip movement chaotic
symmetry ↓
temporal overlap of actions ↑

👉 Translation:

both attacking + reacting simultaneously
🧍 7. AGGRESSIVE ADVANCE
Signal:
A_hip velocity → toward B
shoulders oriented toward B
step frequency ↑
inter_person_distance ↓
🏃 8. RETREAT / DEFENSIVE MOVEMENT
Signal:
hip velocity away from opponent
head leaning backward
feet stepping back rapidly
🧠 9. INTIMIDATION (PRE-CONTACT)
Signal:
inter_person_distance low
no strike yet
shoulders squared
head facing opponent
low but tense movement
💥 10. FALL / LOSS OF BALANCE
Signal:
hip height ↓ suddenly
ankle positions unstable
center of mass shifts outside base
🔁 TEMPORAL LAYER (THIS IS WHERE YOUR MODEL WINS)

Single frame = useless
Sequence = gold

Detect patterns like:
[strike → reaction → counter-strike]
[push → stumble → regain balance]
[grab → pull → resist]
🧪 FINAL FEATURE GROUPING
🟢 Attack signals
wrist velocity spikes
ankle velocity spikes
elbow/knee extension
🟡 Interaction signals
wrist-to-opponent distance
ankle-to-opponent distance
inter-person distance
🔵 Control signals
sustained proximity (grab)
synchronized movement (grapple)
🔴 Chaos signals
acceleration variance
asymmetry
overlapping actions
⚠️ Brutal truth (don’t ignore this)

If you ONLY detect:

“wrist near opponent”

You will fail.

Because:

handshake
helping someone
sports
dancing

👉 You NEED:

velocity + proximity + intent over time
🧭 Clean decision logic (simplified)
if high_velocity_contact and repeated:
    → fight likely

elif sustained_contact and control:
    → grab / restraint

elif low_velocity + forward force:
    → push

elif high ankle velocity + contact:
    → kick
